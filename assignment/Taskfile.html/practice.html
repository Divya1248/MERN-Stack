    What is Netflix ?

    Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies without commercials on an internet-connected device

    How does it work?

    1. Sign Up with Netflix.
    2. Choose a triple play package which will include television, phone, and internet.
    3. After a certain period of time span maybe a month or two you'll receive an email by Verizon's for free Netflix.
    4. Login and enjoy your Netflix.
